The first event of gymnastics for women is vault. You run towards the horse and punch a board and flip yourself over the horse.

The second event is bars. Gymnasts hold on to these wooden poles and flipd from one bar to another.

Beam is the third event and in most eyes, it is the most difficult. It is 4 inches wide and 4 feet off of the ground. The key is to not fall off.

Floor is the 4th and final event of womens gymmnastics. It is also the event where you express yourself the most. You have your own floor music and dance around while doing tumbling passes.

The main goal of gymnastics is to win that gold medal. Sometimes you compete against many gymnasts and only the best will prevail.

The coaches are the most important part of gymnastics. They will either make or break you. Gymnastics coaches end up becoming part of your family. You spend so much time with them and they tech you many life lessons.

Gymastics in general teaches you many life lessons. You learn grit, respect and perseverance.
